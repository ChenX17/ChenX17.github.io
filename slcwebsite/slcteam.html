Satoshi Nakamura

Professor

Dr. Satoshi Nakamura is an IEEE Fellow, ISCA Fellow, Information Processing Society of Japan Fellow, and Advanced Telecommunications Research Institute (ATR) Fellow. His research interests include spoken language processing modeling and systems, speech processing, spoken language translation, spoken dialog systems, natural language processing, and data science. He is one of the world leaders in speech-to-speech translation research.
